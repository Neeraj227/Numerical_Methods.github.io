Why Numerical Ninjas?

Numerical methods are a rapid and efficient solution for mathematical problems. In various engineering fields, these methods provide approximated solutions, offering speed and ease of programming.

Numerical analysis is instrumental in addressing a multitude of issues, including:

  1. Solving linear/non-linear equations and finding real roots through methods like Bisection, Newton-Raphson, etc.
  2. Fitting points to a curve, offering a good approximation and a straightforward solution.
  3. Interpolation, enabling the retrieval of values between a table of values. It's particularly useful for equally spaced readings in unequally spaced methods, with Newton's general method being a key approach.
  4. Solving definite integration using methods that approximate the area under a given curve with great precision. Techniques like Simpson’s rule are commonly employed.
  5. Solving initial value 1st and 2nd order differential equations, providing a good approximation and simplicity compared to traditional analysis.
  6. Optimization problems, where numerical methods help find the maximum or minimum of a function.
  7. Time-series analysis, offering efficient tools for analyzing and forecasting trends in data over time.
